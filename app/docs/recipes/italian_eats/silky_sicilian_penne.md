## Overview

If you’re mad for mushrooms, a fan of fungi, or crazy for creminis, this pasta will treat you right: It’s loaded with ’shroom slices! They’re the flavor backbone of this recipe along with rich mushroom stock, bringing all those deep, earthy flavors that you know and love. We’ve added al dente penne, zucchini and tomatoes, a lemony, chive-flecked crème fraîche sauce, and a sprinkling of Parmesan cheese. Get ready for this umami-packed dish to hit your dinner table.

This meal is inspired by [HelloFresh], which is what we originally used to make this in the past.

> - **Serves:** 6  
> - **Prep Time:** ~10 minutes  
> - **Cook Time:** ~35 minutes

## Ingredients

### Produce

- 3 zucchinis  
- 12 oz Cremini mushrooms  
- 12 oz grape tomatoes  
- ¾ oz chives  
- 3 lemons

### Pantry

- 18 oz penne pasta  
- 3 tbsp Italian Seasoning  
- 16.5 oz mushroom stock concentrate  
- Olive oil  
- Salt & pepper

### Dairy

- 12 tbsp crème fraîche  
- ¾ cup Parmesan cheese  
- 4.5 tbsp butter

## Instructions

### Step 1: Prep Ingredients

- Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  
- Wash and dry produce.  
- Trim and quarter zucchini lengthwise, then cut into ½‑inch pieces.  
- Trim and slice mushrooms. Halve tomatoes. Mince chives. Quarter lemon.

### Step 2: Cook Zucchini

- Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.  
- Add zucchini; season with salt and pepper.  
- Cook, stirring occasionally, until browned and tender, 4–6 minutes.  
- Transfer to a bowl.

### Step 3: Cook Mushrooms

- In same pan, heat another drizzle of olive oil.  
- Add mushrooms; cook until browned and slightly crispy, 5–7 minutes.  
- Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl with zucchini.  
- Wash out pan.

### Step 4: Cook Pasta

- Add penne to boiling water. Cook until al dente, 9–11 minutes.  
- Reserve 2 cups pasta water, then drain.

### Step 5: Make Sauce

- Heat 1.5 tbsp butter and a drizzle of olive oil in pan over medium-high heat.  
- Add tomatoes, 3 tsp Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.  
- Cook until softened, 2–4 minutes.  
- Stir in half the chives and juice from half a lemon.  
- Reduce heat to low. Add stock concentrate and ½ cup pasta water.  
- Whisk in crème fraîche. Season to taste.

### Step 6: Combine and Serve

- Stir in cooked penne, zucchini, mushrooms, and 3 tbsp butter until combined.  
- Add more pasta water as needed to loosen sauce.  
- Divide between bowls. Top with Parmesan and remaining chives.  
- Serve with lemon wedges on the side.
